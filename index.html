HOW IT WORKS

FoodAdvisor provides the traceability of food, allowing manufacturers, distributors and retailers of food to show the quality of the food and communicate the product's origin. Our application, through the use of QR-code , gives buyers a visual tracking of the entire chain on a map and data of interest on food. For producers this can be a benefit showing the quality of its suppliers, communicating the origin of its products and the ingredients in them, to tell their story as a quality guarantee. This creates transparency and enhances the confidence of the customer for a brand. Created to emphasize the authenticity of a product our app will only benefit your enterprise, therefore we invite you to collaborate in our project.


What is a QR Code?

A QR code) is a two-dimensional bar code (or 2D code), ie matrix, composed of blacks modules arranged in a square pattern.

Difference between Barcodes and QR code

Both of the systems used to capture information through automated systems. The barcode, born in the early 70s, is represented by a black vertical rectangle composed of bars of different thickness separated by white lines. 
The QR code is represented in the form of square and consists of a matrix of small white squares and blacks (to the side).
The two encodings are born in industrial areas then establishing itself in different fields. The barcode is stated in the moment in which it is used to automate the management of items for sale in supermarkets. The second is used in principle for the identification of parts in automobile factories.        Aside from the obvious graphical differences, the main difference is the amount of stored information. If you could only store with barcode codes, with the QRCode you can store links, personal data, whole messages and images.        the QR code has the ability to be easily read by smartphones.        The main advantage is just that. To scan a link or contact information on your smartphone simply frame it and scan it using the video camera and special software distributed free also.
